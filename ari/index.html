What is this place? Has your friend ever asks you " what's going on this weekend?" have you ever wanted to simply go out and listen to music and dance like all adults do on Friday and Saturday nights? Well look no further. "Midnight" is a nonprofit moc 9:30 club for teens of the ages 15-18. Experience live music, non-alcoholic drinks and dancing with other attractions waiting on the inside.
 Attraction to the young crowd: though many teens don't want to be treated as young adults this gives you the opportunity to be in an adult setting without the pressure of drinking or being under the influence. We offer a wide range of bands, music with professional DJ's, non-alcoholic drinks and entertainment inside for those who are a bit shyer.  We also would like to stress the fact that no one under the age of 15 or above the age of 18 will be allowed to enter. This is strictly a teen club with full security.
